Introduction

NEWWAVE

NEWWAVE is a magazine focused on the history and music of the New Wave era. It talks about the artists that became pinnicle in the genre, to similar music that took inspiration from it, to random facts about new wave. Its dark, its fun, and its complex. Need good recommendations in the New Wave genre or other music you may like related to it? NEWWAVE has you covered.
